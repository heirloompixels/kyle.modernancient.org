A selection of works gathered together. Strange amalgamations of objects. Found Objects. Found wood. Fine oil paintings. Etchings.

Much of my work is done in series allowing groupings to be displayed as a whole, elaborating on themese. Taken together the pieces are self referential. Interconnected themes emerge and stories are teased out.

I create clouds out of my works. Arranging them as they exist in an interconnected sphere. Finding similarites. Tellings stories. Speaking of the past and the future all at once, as one.
